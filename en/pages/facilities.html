Our Facilities

Comfort and convenience in a privileged environment

Hotel Facilities

At Villarromana we have taken care of every detail to make your stay perfect. Our facilities combine rural charm with all modern comforts.

Rooms

All our rooms are designed to offer you maximum comfort. Decorated in an elegant rustic style that combines tradition with modern amenities.

  • Individual air conditioning and heating
  • Free high-speed WiFi 5G
  • Smart TV with international channels
  • Private bathroom with eco-friendly amenities
  • Safe and minibar
  • Garden or mountain views

Lounge with Fireplace

Our cozy lounge with fireplace is the perfect place to relax after a day exploring the region. A common space to share experiences with other guests.

  • Traditional wood fireplace
  • Library with books and board games
  • Reading area with comfortable armchairs
  • Tea and coffee service all day
  • High-speed WiFi 5G
  • Relaxing ambient music

Terrace and Gardens

Enjoy our outdoor spaces, perfect for disconnecting and connecting with nature. The terrace offers spectacular views of the valley and surrounding mountains.

  • Panoramic terrace with outdoor furniture
  • Gardens with native species
  • Hammock and sun lounger area
  • Pergola with shaded area
  • BBQ available on request
  • Ambient night lighting

Dining Room

Our dining room combines traditional architecture with a welcoming atmosphere. Here you can taste the best local products in our buffet breakfasts.

  • Buffet breakfast with local products
  • Vegetarian and vegan options
  • Dinner service by reservation
  • Special menus for celiacs
  • Regional wines
  • Space for private events

Additional Services

At Villarromana we think of all your needs. We offer a variety of additional services to make your stay even more comfortable and memorable.

  • Free private parking
  • Laundry service
  • Bicycle rental
  • Tourist information and reservations
  • Pets allowed (please inquire)
  • Airport transfers (surcharge)

Accessibility

Our commitment is that all our guests enjoy a comfortable stay. Therefore, we have facilities adapted for people with reduced mobility.

  • Adapted rooms on ground floor
  • Accessible bathrooms with grab bars
  • Access ramps in all areas
  • Elevator to all floors
  • Reserved parking
  • Staff trained in inclusive care
