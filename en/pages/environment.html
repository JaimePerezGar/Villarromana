Natural Environment

A paradise of biodiversity and tranquility

Pure Nature

Villarromana is located on an estate of more than 50 hectares where nature is the protagonist. Centuries-old forests of oaks and holm oaks, natural meadows, crystal-clear streams and rich native fauna create a unique ecosystem that invites disconnection and contact with the natural environment.
